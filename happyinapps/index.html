Who We Are

HappyIn was created in 2024 with the aim of making it easier for people to submit complaints related to health services online. Users can report problems such as complaints of illness, complaints of violence, the nearest health facilities, and follow the development of community needs.

Authorities can respond quickly, thus creating transparency and improving the quality of health services. This application is a bridge between the community and service providers to create a more responsive and inclusive health system.

HappyIn Offer Different Services To Improve Your Health

Supporting your health through a variety of services designed to meet your health needs, from health consultations to lifestyle guidance, our services help you achieve well-being easily and conveniently.

General Consultations

General consultations are available, designed to address a range of health issues, ensuring a comprehensive and personalised approach to people’s needs.

Disease Detection

Use the Disease Detection feature to help recognize early symptoms of various health conditions. Based on your answers to several questions, we will provide advice on the next steps.

Psychologist Consultation

Get mental health support through the Psychologist Consultation feature. Talk to a professional psychologist to overcome stress, anxiety, and various emotional problems with guaranteed privacy.

Emergency Assisten

The Emergency feature is designed to help you in a medical emergency. With one click, you can call the nearest emergency services or ambulance, ensuring a quick response when needed. This app also supports disabilities.

Report Problems

The Report feature allows you to report complaints or problems related to health services directly, helping us to always improve the quality of service according to your needs.

Pharmacy

Access the Pharmacy service to get information and order medicines easily. Find medicines according to prescriptions or doctor recommendations quickly, including availability information at the nearest pharmacy.
